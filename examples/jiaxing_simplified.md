# Example: Jiaxing Regional Scan (Simplified)

This is a simplified public example. It demonstrates the output structure, not a full due-diligence report.

## Short Conclusion

Jiaxing is an advanced-manufacturing city. Its strongest signals are:

1. Photovoltaics / new energy
2. New materials
3. Integrated circuits / power semiconductors

Photovoltaics has the clearest current scale and enterprise chain. New materials has the strongest broad policy positioning and listed-company base. Integrated circuits is smaller but strategically important.
