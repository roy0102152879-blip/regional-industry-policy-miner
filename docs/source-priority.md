# Source Priority

Use primary and near-primary sources first.

## Recommended Source Order

1. Official government documents and full-text policies.
2. Development reform, industry, science, commerce, finance, park, and project-signing sources.
3. Listed-company filings, prospectuses, exchange announcements, and annual reports.
4. Authoritative media and industry associations.
5. PKULaw / 北大法宝 and similar legal-policy databases as secondary sources.

## PKULaw / 北大法宝

PKULaw and similar databases are useful for:

- policy discovery
- abstract-level screening
- policy hierarchy confirmation
- citation-chain tracing
- finding policy names when local government search is weak

But do not treat a policy abstract as a replacement for full text when the claim involves:

- money
- land
- project investment
- enterprise lists
- quantitative targets
- implementation obligations

For those, return to the original official document whenever possible.
